Atomic Design

As the first designer at Intellegens, I tackled improvements for our main product, Alchemite. Early on, I noticed our engineers struggled without a unified design system.

This case study reveals how we built a practical design system, merging UX and AI. Our goal? To make work smoother for everyone and boost our product's quality. Follow our journey from idea to implementation, where simplicity and usefulness were our guiding principles.

challenge.

Developers found it challenging to build features that were consistent without a design system.

auditing.

I started by auditing the components on each page throughout Alchemite to assess our level of consistency.

Audit of existing components

requirements.

What were the key findings from the audit?

The main problem was that whenever a new feature needed a component, like a toolbar, engineers built it from scratch every time. Even though they used Tailwind for styling, each engineer made the feature slightly differently, leading to inconsistency.

business goals.

The business aimed to deliver features faster and more consistently to keep both new and existing customers engaged. Therefore, enhancing development speed became the primary goal of this project.

research.

after defining the requirements and business goals, I started researching design systems and methods to integrate them into teams.

requirements.

why not use an existing design system?

After researching existing design systems and consulting with the development team, we concluded that we wanted to gradually implement our own design system. This approach would afford us greater flexibility than relying on existing ones.

design.

how i created and shared the designs with the engineering team.

selecting designs.

Going forward, we established a process for adding new components to the design system. For existing components, we agreed to select the one we wanted to use, and then I would integrate it into the Figma file.

creating new components..

When creating a new component, we would first identify the component needed. I would then propose designs using Tailwindâ€™s styling, which the team would review and agree upon. Once finalized, I added the design to the Figma file, and the engineers would implement it during our UI sessions.

result.

as a result, we significantly improved the consistency of features and accelerated the development process.
