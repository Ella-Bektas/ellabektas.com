it's great to meet you i'm ella, ux/ui designer and lover of all things product!

I launched my career as a UX designer in 2022, fresh out of Lancaster University with a Design Degree under my belt. Since then, I've been deeply entrenched in the tech realm, contributing my expertise to a leading Cambridge-based applied Machine Learning company.

Working hand in hand with engineers, I've become an advocate for lean UX practices. By seamlessly integrating these methods into agile development teams, I champion continuous delivery, ultimately enhancing user experiences.

why design?

My journey into design began long before university. I had a deep passion for art, finding excitement in every creative endeavor. I even took my GCSE art a year early, earning an A* grade, and continued through to A levels. When it came time to apply for university, I stumbled upon a course I'd never heard of - Design. Until then, I'd only associated design with physical products or interiors, never considering its digital realm. However, as I delved into the course specifications, I had a revelation - design offered an opportunity to creatively solve problems.

why ux/ui design?

After stepping into the world of design at University, I was determined to become an exceptional problem-solver, leveraging my creative skills to address both user and business challenges. In my second year, I enrolled in a module called Interaction Design, marking my first introduction to User Experience (UX) and Interface Design. Through this module, I delved into the intricacies of interaction design. Continuing my exploration, I advanced to the next level in my second year, immersing myself in the advanced module.
